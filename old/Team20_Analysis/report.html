Within the scope of our project, the sections that will be computerized will be the report generation, the verification process, the records system, and the billing process. The data will be stored on the disk and the data center. The hardware that will be needed is a disk, terminal, and card scanner. The ChocAn system will interact with Acme accounting software through the records system, and the reports will interact via an outside emailing system. The provider, manager, and operator will interact with the ChocAn system via their respective terminals. We assumed that the operators and managers also need to be verified in a similar manner to providers and members.
